    Objects & Spaces

    We'll discover the tools and technologies to craft digital experiences in XR, and to transform physical spaces with projection mapping.

    Spacial and XR design remain nascent:

    Mouse & Screen:
    40 years    		 Touch & Mobile:
    15 years    		 Virtual Reality:
    < 8 years    		 Augmented Reality:
    < 5 years

    Spacial and XR interactions remain rare; there are no widespread conventions.

    • AR & Reality Composer: augmenting reality with digital objects.
    • 3D scanning: bringing real-world objects to digital spaces.
    • Blender Basics: editing and manipulating 3D assets.
    • VR & A-Frame: crafting virtual spaces on the web.
    • Projecting Mapping: using objects and spaces as digital canvases.
